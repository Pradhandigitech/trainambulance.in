What Is a Train Ambulance Service?

 

A train ambulance service combines the long-distance travel capability of Indian Railways with the medical monitoring of an ICU ambulance. The patient travels in a reserved coupe or cabin that is converted into a mobile ICU setup, accompanied by trained paramedical staff, medical equipment, and — when required — a doctor. This option is typically chosen for long-distance transfers (500 km and above) where flying is not feasible or affordable, and a road ambulance would take too long or be too uncomfortable for the patient's condition.
