As dawn broke, our expert EMT, Niranjan Kumar, arrived at the hospital, fully prepared for the mission. The team ensured that Ms. Chandrawati was stabilized before departure. She was gently transferred from her hospital bed to the waiting ambulance, which took her to the railway station, where her pre-arranged medical berth awaited her on a high-speed express train.

 

Throughout the journey, Niranjan Kumar remained vigilant, monitoring her vitals, administering medications, and ensuring she was as comfortable as possible. Still, this journey came with challenges.

 

A Safe Arrival
