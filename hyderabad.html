Frequently Asked Questions

 

How much does a train ambulance cost in Hyderabad?

It depends on the route and what your patient needs medically. Short routes like Hyderabad to Bangalore or Chennai start from around ₹60,000–₹75,000. Long-distance routes like Hyderabad to Delhi or Patna range from ₹65,000–₹95,000. Call us — we will give you a specific number in the same conversation, free of charge.

 

How quickly can you arrange a train ambulance from Hyderabad?

Once you call, we start immediately. Depending on the next train available on your route, we typically arrange transfers within 6–24 hours. For urgent cases we check all available options and tell you the fastest realistic timeline — not what you want to hear, what is actually possible.

 

Can family members travel with the patient?

Yes. Family members can travel in the same coach. We help with seating coordination so your family stays close throughout the journey. You do not have to be separated from your loved one.

 

Is train transfer safe for critical patients?

For the right patient — yes, it is often safer than a long road journey. Trains are stable and spacious with consistent power for medical equipment. Our onboard ICU specialist or EMT monitors continuously. We assess every patient before confirming a transfer to make sure train is medically appropriate for their condition.

 

What happens if the patient's condition gets worse during the journey?

Our staff carries emergency medications, a defibrillator, and resuscitation equipment on every journey. They are trained for in-transit emergencies. If a serious situation arises, they manage it onboard and coordinate with the nearest station for ground support. Your patient is never without care at any point.

 

Do you provide a road ambulance at both ends?

Yes — always. It is part of our standard service. Pickup from hospital or home in Hyderabad, delivery to hospital or home at the destination. You do not need to arrange anything separately.

 

Can you bring a patient to Hyderabad from another city?

Absolutely. We regularly coordinate transfers from across India into Hyderabad for treatment at Apollo, AIG, KIMS, Continental, and Yashoda. With offices in Patna, Delhi NCR, and Bangalore we handle the origin-side pickup from multiple cities.

 

What is the difference between your service and an air ambulance?

Air ambulance is faster and necessary for some conditions. Train ambulance is significantly more affordable, better suited for patients who need stable long-duration care, and appropriate for patients where air travel is contraindicated. If you are unsure which is right, call us — we will tell you honestly
