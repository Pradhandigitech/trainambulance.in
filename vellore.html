Train Ambulance in Vellore: How It Works

 
1. Contact and Assessment:

The process begins with you contacting our team via phone or our website. Our medical experts conduct a thorough assessment of the patient's condition to determine the appropriate level of care and equipment required for the journey.

2. Planning and Coordination:

Once the assessment is complete, we plan the entire transfer process. This includes selecting the right train, arranging medical equipment, and coordinating with railway authorities and hospitals. We handle all paperwork and logistical arrangements.

3. Medical Team and Equipment:

A dedicated medical team is assigned to the patient, equipped with all necessary medical supplies and equipment. The team ensures continuous monitoring and care throughout the journey.

4. Transfer and Monitoring:

On the day of the transfer, our team escorts the patient from the hospital/home to the train. During the journey, the medical team provides ongoing care and monitoring to ensure the patient’s stability and comfort.

5. Arrival and Handover:

Upon reaching the destination, the patient is safely transferred to the receiving hospital or healthcare facility. Our team ensures a smooth handover, providing all necessary medical information to the receiving medical staff.
