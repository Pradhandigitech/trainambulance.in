What is a Train Ambulance?

 

A Train Ambulance is a specially equipped train compartment designed to transport patients who need medical attention during their journey. These ambulances are fitted with advanced medical equipment and are staffed by trained medical professionals, ensuring that the patient receives continuous care throughout the trip. Train Ambulance services are particularly beneficial for long-distance transfers where road or air ambulance services might not be feasible due to cost or logistical reasons.
