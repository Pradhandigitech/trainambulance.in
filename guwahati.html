Intoduction about Train Ambulance?

 

A Train Ambulance is a specially equipped rail coach designed to transport patients over long distances while providing comprehensive medical care. These ambulances are staffed with trained medical professionals and equipped with advanced life support systems to ensure that patients receive the necessary care throughout their journey.
